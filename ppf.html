STEK DYNOshield

Paint Protection Film

Military-grade polyurethane film that absorbs the abuse your paint can't. Self-healing technology, 10-year warranty, and installation precision that separates us from the rest.

Important: If your vehicle already has a ceramic coating applied, it must be polished off prior to PPF installation — as the coating prevents the film from bonding properly. A $100 removal fee applies. Please mention this when booking.

About STEK DYNOshield

The Best-Performing Film On the Market

DYNOshield's nano-glass top coat is bonded at a molecular level during manufacturing — virtually eliminating the need for waxes or aftermarket coatings. It delivers unmatched clarity, superior hydrophobicity, and self-healing properties all in a single film.

PPF is a sacrificial layer — it takes the abuse so your factory paint doesn't have to. Rock chips, road debris, light scratches, bug acids, and road salt never reach your paint. When the film takes damage, it heals itself with heat.

Self-Healing TechnologyLight surface scratches and swirls disappear on their own with heat or warm water.
10-Year WarrantyDYNOshield is warrantied against delamination, yellowing, bubbling and cracking for a full decade.
Anti-Contamination SurfaceHydrophobic top coat makes contaminants easy to remove. Keeps the car looking cleaner for longer.
Stain ResistantNano-glass top coat prevents staining from road oils, bugs, and environmental fallout.
