Ceramic Pro Nano-Coatings

What Is Ceramic Pro?

Not a Wax. Not a Sealant. Permanent.

Ceramic Pro 9H is a permanent nanoceramic protective paint coating. Its transparent 3D matrix structure bonds directly to your vehicle's paint at a molecular level — it doesn't sit on top of the surface, it becomes part of it.

Unlike waxes and sealants that leave a film and fade within months, Ceramic Pro can only be removed by machine polishing. Once cured, it creates a surface that stays cleaner longer, resists contamination, and delivers a depth of gloss no other product can match.

9H+ Hardness The highest rating on the pencil hardness scale. Resists light stone chips and everyday scratches.
Unmatched Hydrophobicity Rain causes water to bead and roll off, carrying dirt with it. A drive in the rain leaves your car looking freshly washed.
Thermal Resistance up to 750°C Withstands extreme heat and protects against oxidation and premature fading.
SGS-Certified & REACH Compliant Tested by the world's leading verification company. Safe for humans and the environment.
